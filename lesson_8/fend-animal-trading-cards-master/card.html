Penguin

Penguins range between 4 feet and 16 inches in height depending on the species

  • Scientific name: Pinguinus impennis
  • Fastest species: Gentoo Penguin (22mph)
  • Earliest known penguin: 60 million years ago
  • Fear of human: No

Penguins do not fear humans since most of their harm come from inside the ocean. They can range from 16 inches to 4 feet in height, and they can swim up to 22mph.
